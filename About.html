Our Story

Umbrellas restaurant was created as a form of the Westerhall Rums experience. The brand wanted a location that it could share its products in an exciting way - what other way than a beach side restaurant and bar emulating the epitome of Caribbean culture by way of the beach, good rum, and good vibes. Years before Umbrellas existed, one of the owner's mother had a small bar named "The Umbrella Bar" which operated at the same location. This inspired the name, and expanded the story and evolution of what is now today Umbrellas.
